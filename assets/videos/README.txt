HOW TO REPLACE THE HERO VIDEO
==============================
1. Record or obtain a romantic pre-wedding video clip (MP4 format recommended).
2. Compress it to under 15MB for fast loading (use HandBrake or ffmpeg).
3. Rename the file to: hero.mp4
4. Place it in this folder: assets/videos/hero.mp4
5. The site will automatically use it as the hero background.

For best results:
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- Duration: 15-30 seconds (looped)
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Remove audio or keep it muted (the video plays muted)
