✦ The Artist Behind the Lens

Hi, I'm Sarthak.
I photograph love stories.

What began as a childhood fascination with light and shadow has evolved into an eight-year journey of telling love stories through the lens. I founded ImageStore with one belief: every couple deserves photographs as extraordinary as their relationship.

My approach blends cinematic storytelling with a photojournalist's instinct — I set the stage, then let emotions unfold naturally. The best frames are never posed; they're felt. Whether it's a palace in Rajasthan, a Goan beach at dusk, or a misty Himalayan valley, I find the poetry in every setting.

Over 500 couples have trusted me with their most intimate moments. That trust is something I carry into every shoot — with Sony full-frame cameras, DJI drones, and a team of passionate creatives who share my obsession for the perfect frame.

— Sarthak
